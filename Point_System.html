// HOW THE POINTS WORK

Three rules run the whole board

RULE 01

Effort & Intensity

Activities that demand more from your body pay out more per point. The harder the sport leans on endurance and strength, the bigger the number.

Running → 11/km · Wrestling → 90/hr
RULE 02

Time Commitment

Most activities are scored hourly, so points track the time you actually put in — not just the outcome.

Swimming → 90/hr · Tennis → 81/hr
RULE 03

Inclusiveness

The board isn't just cardio. Strength, mind sports, and team play all earn a seat — everyone finds a way onto the scoreboard.

Endurance · Strength · Team · Mind

Why distance instead of time?

For Running, Cycling, and Walking, points are awarded per kilometer instead of per hour. Distance is a steadier measure of effort — pace varies a lot from person to person, but a kilometer covered is a kilometer earned.

The bigger goal

  • Promote healthy habits
  • Encourage consistent participation
  • Recognize diverse interests and abilities
  • Build a culture of wellness and engagement
